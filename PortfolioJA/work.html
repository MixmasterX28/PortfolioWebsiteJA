001 : Tefnut's Pantheon

Tefnut's Pantheon is a 2D platform fighter inspired by games like Super Smash Bros. and Brawlhalla, but with a twist: the stages have walls and the fighters have health bars.

This was my first group project, where I collaborated with other game developers and artists to create a complete game. Our task was to choose a genre and we were then randomly assigned a myth to base the game on, which in our case was the Egyptian goddess Tefnut. We combined these elements to develop a unique concept around that theme.

My main contributions included developing the movement system, handling running, jumping, and character control, as well as implementing the art assets provided by the artists and ensuring they integrated smoothly with the gameplay systems. In addition to these core tasks, I also offered support wherever needed and taught the art team how to use GitHub effectively for version control.

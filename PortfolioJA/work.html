001 : Tefnut's Pantheon

Tefnut's pantheon is a 2d platform fighter, a game comparable to the inspirations we took it from, those inspiration were super smash bro's and brawlhalla.

This was my first group project where i worked together with other game developer and game artist to make the game, We got tasked to pick a genre and then proceeded to get a randomly assigned myth along with it which was the myth of the egyptic got tefnut and we were then supposed to make a game out of these two concepts.

my work in this project consisted of making the movement system, i made the running and jumping and i also was in charge of implementing the different art assets we got from the game artists and making them work with everything, along these main tasks i also generally provided help where it was needed and taught the game artists how to work with github.

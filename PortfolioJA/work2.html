002 : Eyes of evil

Eyes of evil is a simple tower defense game with the premise being that you have full control over the towers instead of them firing by themselves. you choose when the towers attack and you can also help your towers out by using an attack that is centered around your mouse button. this project was made for an assignment and as practice for my c# skills.

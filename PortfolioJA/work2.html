002 : Eyes of evil

Eye's of Evil is a simple tower defense game. The main premise is that all the towers are controlled by the player instead of firing automatically. You also have the ability to cast a targeted strike with your mouse to help out during the downtime when your towers are on cooldown.

This was a solo project where most of the assets were made by me, and all the code was written by me. I had a lot of fun developing it, as I had complete creative freedom and was able to experiment with different ideas.
